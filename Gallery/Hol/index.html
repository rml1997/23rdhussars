  • [Memorial, Ijsselstein Church]
 The Regiment arrived here on 19 Nov and stayed until 28 Nov.

The Memorial lists those allied servicemen who died fighting in the area. It includes 23H man Dennis Gerrard Manders of B Squadron. Unfortunately, his date of death is wrong - he was killed on 21 Nov, not 21 Oct. Furthermore, three other 23H men who were killed the same day are omitted entirely. They are William White, James Raper and John McKinnon Forbes.
