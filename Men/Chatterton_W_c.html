William Chatterton ( )

Soldier
Service No:7882203
Rank:Trooper
Squadron:C
Service:Fought in the Royal Tank Corps in WW1. In WW2 he joined the 2nd Light Tank Company, Royal Tank Corps before moving to 23H.
Prisoner of War:Captured 29 Jun 1944 Held at Stalag VIII-C, Konin Zaganski, Poland
Award(s):Military Medal (Gazetted 8 May 1935) for "bravery in the field during the Mohmand Operations, North West Frontier of India, 15th/16th August to 15th/16th October, 1935".
