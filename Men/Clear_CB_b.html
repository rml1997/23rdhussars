Notes

Corporal Clear was in command of Sherman tank no. T149996 when it took a direct hit during the assault on Hill 112 (see the 23H Story, p57 and Bishop Page 17, Page 18). Although wounded, he managed to escape. The remaining crew members were all killed:

  • L/C R.P. Holt (Driver)
  • Tpr J.A. Stebbings (Co-driver)
  • Tpr J. Brown (Operator)
  • Tpr J.E. Sawyer (Gunner)
