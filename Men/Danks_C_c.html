Notes

Clifford had an elder brother, Benjamin Frederick Danks (born 1896), who fought as a Gunner with The Royal Field Artillery during WWI. He suffered from shell shock and contracted Pulmonary Tuberulosis. He was discharged on 22 Mar 1918, being 'physically unfit', and died on 28 Mar 1919.
