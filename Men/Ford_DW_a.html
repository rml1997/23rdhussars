Notes

On day one of Operation Bluecoat (30 Jul 1944), 23H was approaching the village of Sept Vents in Normandy. The 23H Story states, on Page 83, "Not only did the banks and lanes slow up the tanks but also it was soon found that 'A' Squadron were in a minefield and they did in fact lose two tanks in it". Derek was probably wounded in this incident.

He is on the list of those who attended the 23H reunion in 1959.
