March 1941

Penkridge

DateWar DiaryNotes
1-2Normal training. 
3Normal training. 1 x Medium tank Mark IIX received from VRS, Chilwell. "Our first real, live, running tank". 23H Story Page 15.
4Normal training. Road party moved to Knaresborough.  
6Regiment moved by rail to Knaresborough to take over from 41st Royal Tank Regiment. 5 x Personnel Carriers received from VRS, Chilwell. 4 x BSA Solo Motorcycles received from O.O., Overton-on-Dee. 1 x Vauxhall & 2 x Morris 4-str cars from LTC, Loyals, Preston. 

Knaresborough

7Normal training.It is almost certain that at least one squadron would have been billeted at Scriven Hall in Knaresborough.
8Normal training. 4 x Morris 2-str Utility cars received from VRS, Chilwell. 
9Normal training. Rear party moved to Knaresborough. 
10Normal training. 2/Lieut TR Morley joined.Timothy Richard Morley was the brother of Major (later Brigadier) Michael Frederick Morley MBE, who had joined 23H on 7 Dec 1940.
11-13Normal training. 
14Normal training. 2 x instructors in D & M and 1 in Gunnery sent to Divisional School. 
15-16Normal training. 
17Normal training. Brigade DR School opened. 
18Normal training. 
19Normal training. 1 x D & M Instructor arrived from 2nd R.G.H., 1 Gunnery Instructor arrived from 13th/18th Hussars. 
20Normal training. 2 x tanks, Infantry Mark III, received from 46th Royal Tank Regiment. 
22-23Normal training. 
24Divisional School opened. 12 x HQ Driver/Operators start training. 2/Lieut M. Watney arrived. Michael George Watney would be killed in Tunisia on 29 Mar 1943 whilst attached to the 10th Royal Hussars.
25Normal training. 1 x Gunnery Instructor arrived from 54th Training Regiment. 4 x Austin 2-str Utility cars received from 29th Armoured Brigade. 1 x Bedford 3-ton lorry received from Ministry of Supply. 
26Normal training. 1 x Office truck received from V.R.S. Chilwell. 
27Normal training. Drill competition between HQ recruits and 24th Lancers recruits. 2 x Austin 2-str Utility cars received from 8th Battalion, Rifle Brigade. 
28Normal training. 1 x D & M Instructor arrived from 15th/19th Hussars. 1 x Bedford 3-ton lorry received from Ministry of Supply.  
29-30Normal training. 
31Inspection by Divisional Commander. Major General Percy Hobart.
23H Story Page 16.
