June 1941

Knaresborough

DateWar DiaryNotes
1-2Normal training. 2/Lieut W.G.C. Shebbeare to 29th Armoured Brigade as L.O. .  
4Address by Divisional Commander to all Officers and N.C.O.s in the Brigade.  
5-9Normal training. 
10Regimental Advance Party leaves for Nawton, Nr York, the new training area.  
11Four I tanks Mark III arrived from 1st Gloucester Hussars.  
12-13Normal training. 
14Regiment moved by rail from Knaresborough to Nawton Camp.  

Nawton

15Normal training. 46 x Other Ranks transferred to West Yorkshire Regiment.  
16Rear party reported. Three I tanks Mark III from 1st Northants Yeomanry.  
17Normal training. 
186 x Other Ranks transferred to I.T.C. West Yorkshire Regiment, Twenty Other Ranks drafted from 57th Training Regiment, of whom six were returned as unsuitable.  
19-202 x Other Ranks transferred to I.T.C.West Yorkshire Regiment.  
22One I tank Mark III arrived from 11th Armoured Division School, also one Light Dragon Mark IIB.  
23-28Normal training. 
29Two I tanks Mark III arrivd from C.O.D., Chilwell.  
30"A" squadron camp, 1st crews.
