October 1941

Whitby

 
DateWar DiaryNotes
1-2Firing at Fylingdales ranges. 2 x Scout cars received.  
3-4Rear Division exercise No. 2 for B Echelon. 
5-8Normal training. 2/Lieuts Joiner and Balding joined the Regiment for duty from 102 OCTU. 2 x Matilda III C.S. tanks received. 3 x M.C.s and 1 x Medium Tank Mark II transferred away.  
9-16Exercise "PERCY". 
17-21Normal training. Hillman Utility received. Morris Utility transferred away. 2/Lieuts A.J. Glynn, F.F. Foyle & C. Robinson posted to 53rd Training Regiment for duty with 44th Battalion Recce. Corps.
1-6Normal training. 
22Individual training starts. 
23-31Normal training. 2 x Light Dragons Mark IIB transferred away.
