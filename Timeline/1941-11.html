November 1941

Whitby

DateWar DiaryNotes
1-6Normal training. 2 x Carriers, Tracked, started and charging Mark II received.  
72/Lieut F.J. Shearman joined the Regiment for duty from 102 OCTU. Frederick John Shearman would become Adjutant. 23H Story Page 9.
8-16Normal training. 
17-19Firing at Fylingdales open ranges. 1 x Standard Ironside transferred away.  
20Inter-Regimental tank point-to-point. 
21Visit of G.O.C. Northern Command.  
22-25Normal training. 2/Lieut Gray posted to 61st Training Regiment. Light tank Mark VIB with A.A. Conversion received. Chevrolet 15-cwt truck transferred away.  
26-Dec 5Firing at Linney Head Ranges.
