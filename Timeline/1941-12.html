December 1941

Whitby

DateWar DiaryNotes
53 x M/C.s received. 3 x Impressed lorries transferred away.  
6-16Normal training. 
17Echelon firing at Fylingdales ranges.  
18-30Normal training. Morris utility and Valentine tank Mark IV received. Light tank Mark V and Medium Dragon Mark III transferred away.  
31Lieut J.C. Courtney joined the Regiment for duty from 4 Res A. Tank Regiment.
