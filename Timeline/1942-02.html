Whitby

DateWar DiaryNotes

4Lieut Whitehill appointed Captain. Captain T.D. Wilson MC posted to 57th Training Regiment. Sir Thomas Douglas Wilson became 4th Baronet Wilson in 1968, succeeding his uncle. His father had been killed in 1917 fighting with the Argyll and Sutherland Highlanders.
