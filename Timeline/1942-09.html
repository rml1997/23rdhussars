Thetford

DateWar DiaryNotes
1Mobilisation - Zero Day. 
24 x Scout cars on strength.

4Normal training.

9Squadron training. 2 x Carriers off strength

124 x Crusader III tanks received.  
143 x Crusader II tanks off strength.

20Final conference on exercise BULL. 
24Draft of 4 men from 55th Training Regiment RAC. 1 x Scout Car and 1 x Carrier off strength.

304 x Utilities, 1 x 4-seater car, 9 x 30-cwt lorries, 4 x 3-ton lorries, 4 x 15-cwt trucks and 1 x motor cycle combination off strength.
