August 1943

Bridlington

DateWar DiaryNotes
1-10Firing at Riccal Bridge and Spaunton.  
3Conference on exercise "HAWK". 2 x Bedford 3-tonners received. 8 x Utilities and 21 x t-tonners sent away.  
4Sherman Field Trials. 
7Captain Stephenson posted to M.E.F. Captain John Cory Stephenson was to die in an air crash in Greece, en route to Albania, on 1 Dec 1943.
835 x Other Ranks posted to draft RWYH.  
9-10Rear link exercise. 
11Office truck received from 8th R.B. 18 x Other Ranks taken on strength from draft "REEFER". REEFER was an exchange of men between Home and Middle East units. 23H Story Page 22.
14-16Exercise, Bridlington. 
17Lieut Carlos-Clarke posted to Commando Depot. Professional boxer, dog kennel assistant, vegetable chef and cowboy; a boyhood friend of the Prince of Wales (later Edward VIII), 'Sandy', as he was known, would become a well-known racehorse trainer.
18Mine clearing TEWT. 
19-20Mine clearing exercise. 2 x Office trucks sent away. 
23Signal exercise. 
30-31Divisional exercise. 
312/Lieut Proctor arrived from OCTU. 3 x Daimler Scout Cars received. James Gordon Procter censored one of my Dad's letters.
