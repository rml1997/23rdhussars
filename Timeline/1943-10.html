October 1943

Bridlington

DateWar DiaryNotes
1-10Normal training. 
53 x Daimler Scout Cars received from 11th Armoured Division. 
11-14Wading (All Squadrons). 
124 x Humber Scout Cars and 4 x Trailers, 1-ton received. 28 x Bedford 3-tonners struck off.  
11-17Inspection of AFVs by AFV Inspectorate.  
163 x Daimler Scout Cars transferred to Inns of Court Regiment. 
18Firing at Boynton Range. 
19Firing at Filey Range. 2/Lieut P.S. Holt joined the Regiment. 
21Firing at Boynton Range. G.O.C. visited the Regiment.  
22Advance party to Kirkcudbright. 4 x Humber Scout Cars received. 
23Main party to Kirkcudbright. 
24-Nov 5Firing at Kirkcudbright. 
262 x Daimler Scout Cars struck off.
