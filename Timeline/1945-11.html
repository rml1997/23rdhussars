November, 1945. Husum. 1/100,000 Sheet J3.

DateWar DiaryNotes
1 - 30 No changes in the activities and events reported for October took place except for further exchanges and release of personnel. A total of 94 men were released, of whom 8 went under Class B. Furthermore a total of 121 men were posted, the majority to 7th armoured Division. In exchange 169 men of low age groups arrived, the largest number from 141st R.A.C. who were being broken up. My Dad was posted to the 5th Royal Inniskilling Dragoon Guards on 4th Nov.
