December, 1945. Husum. 1/100,000 Sheet J3.

DateWar DiaryNotes
1 - 30 No changes in the activities and events reported for November took place. A total of 48 men were released Class A and 4 under Class B. Furthermore a total of 86 men were posted into the Regiment and 8 were posted away.

A Security Exercise was held in the last week to test preparations for meeting civilian troubles. No incidents, however, occurred during the month.

The remaining Wehrmacht were disbanded at the end of the month under Operation "Clobber II". A civilian Kreis Pool was however retained consisting of 175, mostly woodcutters.

The Unit also gave support in stimulating German youth activities which slowly got under way.
