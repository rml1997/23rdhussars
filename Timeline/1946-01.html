January, 1946. Husum. 1/100,000 Sheet J3.

DateWar DiaryNotes
1 - 21 A & S Group 24 went out on January 4th consisting of 105 men closely followed on January 9th by A & S Group 25, consisting of 131 men.

On January 4th disbandment orders were received and the Unit was disbanded on January 21st, 1946.
