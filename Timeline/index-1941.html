1941

  • Lots of 'normal training' at Penkridge, Staffs.
  • Move to Knaresborough, Yorkshire in March.
  • The Regiment's first casualty - a man is killed, probably during training.
  • Move to Nawton, Yorkshire in May.
  • The first of many visits to the ranges at Fylingdales.
  • Move to Whitby, Yorkshire in July.
  • Perry Harding takes over from Roscoe Harvey as C.O.
