1944

  • Bridlington. First Stuart ('Honey') tanks arrive in January.
  • Move to Aldershot in March.
  • 'Light party' comes ashore in Normandy, June 13.
  • 'Main party' comes ashore in Normandy, June 15/16.
  • Into battle: OP EPSOM, Normandy, June 26-30.
  • OP GOODWOOD, Normandy, July 18-20.
  • OP BLUECOAT, Normandy, starts July 30.
  • Breakout from Normandy, August.
  • Across the Seine at Vernon and through Amiens, late August.
  • Into Belgium and the vital objective: Antwerp, September.
