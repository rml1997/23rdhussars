1945

  • Regiment formed; based at Teddesley Hall, Penkridge, Staffs.
  • Early intake of officers, the majority from the 10th Royal Hussars.
  • Various assorted vehicles arrive (no tanks).
  • The first 281 recruits arrive.
  • Preparations interrupted by Christman leave.
  • Training starts.
